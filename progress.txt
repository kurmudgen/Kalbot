KALSHI BOT ARCHITECTURE (bot/)
================================================================

Every 5 minutes:
├── Kill switch check (~/kalbot/STOP file)
├── Pre-trade checks:
│   ├── Early exit: sell winners (60% profit), cut losers (30% loss)
│   ├── Polymarket signal: detect sharp PM moves → trade on Kalshi
│   ├── Market maker: spread orders on thin weather markets
│   └── Data sniper: pre-research upcoming CPI/payrolls/FOMC
│
├── Quantitative strategies (each has its own module):
│   ├── SP500 (sp500_strategy.py): VIX-implied brackets, VRP haircut
│   ├── ECON (econ_strategy.py): Cleveland Fed + GDPNow + FedWatch
│   ├── BITCOIN (bitcoin_strategy.py): DVOL + jump-diffusion Monte Carlo
│   ├── TREASURY (treasury_strategy.py): yield change distribution
│   ├── GAS (gas_strategy.py): EIA weekly + crude correlation
│   └── FOREX (forex_strategy.py): implied vol bracket pricing
│
├── Ensemble strategies (local filter → cloud analyst → executor):
│   ├── WEATHER: dual local model filter → Perplexity + Claude + DeepSeek
│   └── WILDCARD: all categories, big mispricings, breaking news
│
├── Ensemble analyst (ensemble_analyst.py):
│   ├── Perplexity researches (live web search)
│   ├── Claude evaluates independently
│   ├── DeepSeek evaluates independently
│   ├── Adaptive Brier-score reweighting per model per category
│   ├── Consensus required (all agree on direction)
│   └── Calibration pipeline: Platt scaling → edge dampening → spread penalty
│
├── Executor (executor.py):
│   ├── Kill switch check
│   ├── Kelly criterion position sizing (quarter-Kelly)
│   ├── Category-specific confidence thresholds
│   ├── Historical bias adjustment (Kalshi YES overpricing)
│   ├── Correlation guard (max 2 per weather city group)
│   ├── Edge dampening (>18% edges are unreliable)
│   └── Paper/live mode
│
├── Strategy rebalancer (strategy_rebalancer.py):
│   ├── Shifts budget toward winning strategies
│   ├── 10% min, 70% max per strategy
│   ├── Rolling 7-day performance window
│   └── Recalculates every cycle
│
├── Auto-scaler (auto_scaler.py):
│   ├── $50 → $200 → $500 → $1,000 as win rate proves out
│   └── Auto-downgrades if recent WR < 55%
│
├── Weekly repo scanner (weekly_repo_scan.py):
│   └── Sundays ~6AM: git pull 15 tracked repos, flag changes
│
└── Default allocations:
    WEATHER 30%, SP500 20%, BITCOIN 12%, WILDCARD 12%,
    ECON 8%, TREASURY 8%, GAS 5%, FOREX 5%

================================================================
KEY FINDINGS FROM REPO ANALYSIS
================================================================

1. Economic AI trades had -70% ROI in ryanfrigo's bot (78% of losses)
   → Our econ strategy requires >12% edge, very conservative
2. NO-side only was the ONLY consistently profitable Kalshi strategy
   → NO_SIDE_PREFERENCE flag added
3. Edge paradox: edges >18% correlate with LOWER win rate
   → Edge dampening at 18% threshold integrated
4. NWS-only recommended for weather (Open-Meteo 2-4F off)
   → Station mapping added, NWS timing multipliers
5. Kalshi markets 20-50c overprice YES by 6-10% (7.3M market analysis)
   → Bias adjustment integrated into executor
6. VIX overestimates actual S&P vol ~60% of the time
   → VRP haircut applied in S&P strategy
7. Cauchy distribution better than normal for S&P/BTC (fat tails)
   → Jump-diffusion Monte Carlo for BTC brackets
